SEAGEM History

Sea Gem Training and Assessment Center started as maritime training center instituted on 2012 and was recognized by Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) No.
0188389. Its office is located at G/F Marbella II Bldg., 2071 Roxas Blvd., Malate, Manila. On its "maiden voyage", Sea Gem catered to the need of the Filipino seafarers when it offered its pioneering course- Free Fall Life Boat Training Course.
Sea Gem is now on its way towards expanding its service in the technical skills development-related trainings. In 2018, Sea Gem ventured a partnership with Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA), to contribute to the development of the Filipino workforce in providing quality technical-educational and skills development for domestic and foreign employment.
Sea Gem adheres to the innovation and continual development in order to meet the needs of Filipino workforce in honing their competency to meet global challenges. Sea Gem emerged from its seedbed nurtured with core values for a steady growth in partnership with related industry.
Guided by high ideals and ambitions, Sea Gem establishes its own identity in gaining the trust and confidence of the various clients.
